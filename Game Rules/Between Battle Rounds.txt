- Between Battles:
  1. Game round takes place.
  2. Fleets can enter the outer-rim without having to join the ongoing battle.  
  3. Fleets can enter the inner-side of the star system(s) where space and/or ground battles take place
      1. If a space battle is still ongoing, the new fleet / must join the battle
      2. The fleet must respect the general space battle rules, including ground reinforcement
  4. Fleets can bring reinforcement that can support the ongoing space / ground battles
  5. [Flagless] fleets can join ongoing battles
  6. [Flagless] fleets can bring ground units to reinforce ground battles.
